**Privacy Policy**

Nitido is an open-source personal finance app (AGPL-3.0). This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page describes our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for anyone who uses our Service.

If you choose to use our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described herein.

The terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms and Conditions.

**Information We Collect**

- **Account information** (email, name): required for Firebase Authentication and cloud sync.
- **Financial transactions**: entered manually by you or auto-imported from banking SMS/notifications. Stored locally and optionally synced to your private Firebase Firestore.
- **SMS messages**: only processed if you grant READ_SMS/RECEIVE_SMS permission for auto-import. Messages are processed locally and raw content is NOT stored.
- **Notifications**: only processed if you enable NotificationListenerService for auto-import. Raw notification text is NOT stored.
- **Audio**: only recorded when you activate AI dictation (button-initiated). Processed on-device and NOT stored.
- **App usage analytics**: collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the Service. You can opt out in Settings → Privacy.
- **Crash reports**: collected via Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs. Disabled in debug mode. You can opt out.
- **Device ID**: auto-generated by Firebase for analytics and crash reporting correlation.

**AI Features**

When you use AI-powered auto-import or chat:

- By default, transaction text is sent to our Nexus AI Gateway (`api.ramsesdb.tech`) for ephemeral categorization. No text is logged beyond rate-limit metadata.
- If you configure your own AI provider key (BYOK mode), text is sent directly to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini. Their privacy policies apply to those requests.

**Third-Party Services**

We use the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies:

- [Firebase (Google)](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics
- [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)

**Data We Do NOT Collect**

- Precise location (no GPS, no IP geolocation)
- Contacts
- Calendar
- Photos or media files
- Health data
- Browsing history
- Social profiles

**Data Sales**

We do NOT sell user data to third parties. We do NOT share user data with advertisers.

**Data Security**

- Data in transit: encrypted via HTTPS (TLS)
- Data at rest: Firestore-managed encryption; local database stored in app-private storage
- API keys: encrypted locally with `FlutterSecureStorage`
- Android backup: disabled — financial data is NOT backed up to Google Drive

**Your Rights**

- **Export your data**: Settings → Export → CSV or JSON format
- **Delete your account**: Settings → Account → Delete account (removes all Firestore data)
- **Opt out of analytics**: Settings → Privacy → Disable analytics
- **Data portability**: Export at any time, in standard formats

**Children's Privacy**

This Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If discovered, such data is immediately deleted.
